Current Obsessions

The album I am listening to most right now is Carrie & Lowell (2015) by Sufjan Stevens. Sufjan Stevens might be my favorite musical artist, along with the Beatles, and his new album is beautiful.

Another amazing new album is To Pimp A Butterfly (2015) by Kendrick Lamar. It is an extremely creative and perfectly executed hip hop album.

I also have to mention the song "Hardware" from Schoolhouse Rock. It is incredible. YouTube video.
